Victoria Bay Resort!

There is something for you in our resort, whether you've come to Albay for business or for leisure. Your experience may include, SWIMMING POOL (with Kiddie Pool and Jacuzzi), INFINITY POOL (with Jacuzzi), INFLATABLE ISLAND

Whether you're swimming laps in the pool or just splashing around with friends and family, it's a fun and healthy activity for all ages. And with the kiddie pool and Jacuzzi available at the resort, everyone can enjoy the water, no matter their age or skill level.

Shop, Dine and book your Meetings with us!

At the resort, you'll find a variety of options for shopping, dining, and meeting spaces. The shop offers a wide range of merchandise, from souvenirs to clothing and accessories, so you can bring home a piece of your trip. The dining options are sure to satisfy any craving, with a variety of cuisines and styles to choose from. Whether you're in the mood for a casual meal or a fine dining experience, the resort has got you covered. And if you're here for work, the meeting spaces are perfect for hosting events, conferences, or even just a small business meeting. With everything you need right here on the resort grounds, you can shop, dine, and conduct business with ease and convenience.
